Refund Policy

Our goal is to ensure transparency in all transactions between Customers and Experts.

Our satisfaction rate is 95%+, and most customers return for more services. However, in rare cases where issues arise, this policy explains how refunds work.

Our Website is a platform that connects Experts and Customers. The Company is not responsible for monitoring individual transactions.

Refunds may be requested if the Product does not meet the specified requirements.

If no Expert is assigned to your Order, you are entitled to a 100% refund.

If an Expert has been assigned and you cancel the Order:

  • Before completion – refund 70%-100% (depends on work done).
  • After completion – reviewed by our Quality Assurance team.

If delivery is late due to our fault, a partial refund may be issued.

No refunds apply if delays are due to incomplete Customer instructions.

Refunds are not issued if:

  • Product was used for personal purposes.
  • Grade was lower than expected (We provide guidance, not guaranteed grades).
  • You were not admitted to an institution.
  • Refund period (6 months) has expired.

If a chargeback is issued through a credit card company, we may suspend access to your account.

Customers are responsible for fees related to chargebacks or payment disputes.

Once approved, refunds are processed within 3 business days.

Refunds cannot be transferred between payment methods.
